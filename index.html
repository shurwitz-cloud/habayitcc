About Habayit

A Jewish Home
in Cooper City

בַּיִת יִשְׂרְאֵלִי בְּקוּפֶּר סִיטִי

Habayit Jewish Center was founded with a single vision: to create a place where every Jewish person — regardless of background, affiliation, or observance — feels genuinely at home.

We offer Shabbat and holiday celebrations, Jewish education for all ages, meaningful life-cycle events, and a warm community that shows up for one another.

כָּל יִשְׂרָאֵל עֲרֵבִים זֶה בָּזֶה "All of Israel is responsible for one another." Talmud · Shevuot 39a
