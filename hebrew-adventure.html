About the Program

A place to learn, belong, and grow.

HaBayit Hebrew Adventure offers children a meaningful, age-appropriate Jewish education in a warm and supportive environment — Hebrew reading, holidays and values, and genuine friendship, taught by caring, experienced educators.

📅 Classes meet every Sunday, 10:00 AM – 12:00 PM.
What Children Experience

Three Pillars of Our Program

Hebrew Reading

Step-by-step Hebrew literacy at every child's own pace.

Holidays & Values

Hands-on learning about the Jewish calendar and its meaning.

Community & Friendship

A nurturing space where every child feels they belong.
