About the Program

A Place to Learn, Belong, and Grow

Habayit Hebrew School offers children a meaningful, age-appropriate Jewish education in a warm and supportive environment. Our students learn to read Hebrew, explore Jewish holidays and values, and build genuine pride in their heritage — all while having fun and making friends.

Classes are taught by caring, experienced educators who bring Torah and tradition to life for young learners.

📅 Classes meet every Sunday, 10:00 AM – 12:00 PM.

Enrollment Policies

Enrollment in Habayit Hebrew School is for the full school year. We are unable to issue refunds or credits for absences due to illness, holidays, family travel, or early withdrawal.

If Hebrew School is canceled on a given day due to weather or another unforeseen circumstance, there will be no make-up sessions, refunds, or credits for that day.

If a card payment cannot be processed due to insufficient funds or decline, a $25 fee may be applied to the account for each failed transaction.

We welcome children who are able to participate successfully in a group classroom setting. If, in the judgment of our program director, a child requires additional support beyond what our program can provide, we will work together with the family to find a suitable path forward.

We may use photographs of your child(ren) in materials, our website, and social media. Children's last names are never published.
